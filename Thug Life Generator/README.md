Generates a Thug Lige image from the given input image.
Overlays a cool spectacles on top of the eyes.

How To Use:

im = imread('test.jpg');

thug = thugLyf(im);

imshow(thug);


Have a look at the test.jpg and the test_result.jpg to see what happens!
